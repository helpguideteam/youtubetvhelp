Activate YouTube on your TV with yt.be/activate

Link your YouTube account to Smart TVs, streaming devices, and game consoles by entering the short activation code at yt.be/activate. Follow our step-by-step guide to complete activation in under five minutes.

Get Started — Activate Now

Quick summary

Use yt.be/activate to enter the activation code shown on your TV screen. Works for Smart TVs, Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, game consoles, and most streaming boxes.

Open yt.be/activate Read FAQ

Step 1 — Open YouTube on your TV

Launch the YouTube app on your Smart TV, streaming device, or console. Navigate to Sign in or Link device from the left menu.

Step 2 — Get the activation code

After choosing Sign in, your TV will show a short activation code (for example AB12-CD34) and instructions to visit yt.be/activate on a phone or computer.

Step 3 — Visit yt.be/activate

On your phone or computer open https://yt.be/activate (or youtube.com/activate), sign in with the Google account you want to link, and enter the code displayed on the TV.

Step 4 — Confirm and finish

After entering the code, confirm the device name (optional) and allow the link. The TV will automatically refresh and show the signed-in profile.

Step 5 — Troubleshoot common issues

If the code expires, request a new code on the TV. If sign-in fails, check network connectivity or sign out of conflicting Google accounts in your browser.

Tips & best practices

  • Use the same Google account on phone/computer and TV for a smooth link.
  • Ensure TV & device are on the same network (not strictly required, but helpful).
  • If using public/shared devices, sign out after use.

  • Streaming devices (Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV)

Troubleshooting — common problems & fixes

Code not working / expired

Activation codes expire quickly for security. On the TV open Sign in again to get a fresh code and enter it immediately on yt.be/activate.

Network or connectivity errors

Restart your TV/router, verify internet access on the TV, and ensure any firewall or DNS filtering isn’t blocking youtube.com.

Wrong Google account signed in

Sign out from other Google accounts in your browser, or use an incognito/private window to sign in with the correct account before entering the code.

TV app outdated

Update the YouTube app on your TV (via the device’s app store) or reinstall it if activation repeatedly fails.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is yt.be/activate?

yt.be/activate is a short URL that takes you to the YouTube activation page (same as youtube.com/activate) where you enter the code shown on your TV to link your account.

Do I need a Google account?

Yes. You must sign in with a Google account (the same one you want to use on the TV) when prompted on yt.be/activate.

Is yt.be/activate safe?

Yes — it’s an official YouTube/Google URL that forwards to the activation flow. Always ensure the site is served over HTTPS and you sign in on a trusted device.

What if I don’t see a code on the TV?

Open the YouTube app’s menu and choose Sign in or Link device. If still not visible, update the app or restart the device.
